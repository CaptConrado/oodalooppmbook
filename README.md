## Project Overview

A first-draft business book applying John Boyd's OODA Loop (Observe, Orient, Decide, Act) to the practice of product management. The book is arguing that competitive advantage in product development is not a function of superior frameworks or smarter people, but of tempo, alignment, and "daring".  (speed and honesty of a team's feedback cycle.) 

Follow the progress here: https://captconrado.github.io/oodalooppmbook/
